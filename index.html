Your way to take care of your lungs

SoundSpiro is an eHealth solution designed for early detection of lungs respiratory dysfunctions such as asthma, bronchitis, COVID-19 lungs complications, etc. along with health enthusiasts wishing to monitor their lungs condition. SoundSpiro user-friendly app enables monitoring your lungs performance values, observe their dynamic over time and compares results with the previous tests.
